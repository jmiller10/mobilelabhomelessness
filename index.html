Volunteers





If you would like to receive information regarding volunteer opportunities, please leave your contact information. You will be notified of opportunities that you may be interested in that are in your area.






Seeking Services

Homeless Resources



GLIDE - Free Meals DAILY



GLIDE provides free meals with the help of kitchen and security staff. There are also volunteers that help serve food. Breakfast, lunch, and dinner are served daily. The poor, homeless, and hungry are welcomed.


For More Information, Visit Their Site


Saint Vincent De Paul Society - MSC Shelter



The MSC Shelter offers a place to stay, and provides several other services, including employment and education. The shelter is run by a dedicated staff along with a stable volunteer group. The goal is for those who access the shelter to be able to make positive changes to their lives.


For More Information, Visit Their Site
